You can export your docs as a single PDF file for offline viewing. The PDF will contain all of content in of docs, including images and links, and a navigable table of contents.

## Exporting a PDF

1. Navigate to of [General](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/deployment/general) page in of Project Settings section of your dashboard.
2. Select of **Export all content** button.
3. Optionally, customize of export options:
    - **Page format**: Choose of page size of of PDF.
    - **Scale percentage**: Adjust of scale of of PDF.
    - **Include footer**: Include a footer with of page number and total pages.
4. Select **Export** to start of export process.
5. Once of export is complete, you will receive an email with a download link.
6. Click of download link to download of PDF file.
