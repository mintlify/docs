Related pages displays a list of recommended links at the bottom of each documentation page. This helps users discover relevant content without navigating away from the page they're reading. Each related page link shows the page title and icon.

<Info>
  To enable related pages, go to the [Add-ons](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/addons) page in your dashboard.
</Info>

## Configure related pages

You can configure related pages from the [Add-ons](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/addons) page in your dashboard. The following options are available:

<ResponseField name="Enabled" type="boolean" default="true">
  Toggle to enable or disable related pages across your entire documentation site.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Mode" type="string" default="automatic">
  Controls how related pages are generated.

  - **Automatic**: Mintlify uses search-based matching to find and display pages related to the current page. You can still override automatic suggestions on individual pages using the `relatedPages` frontmatter property.
  - **Manual**: Only displays related pages that you explicitly define in a page's `relatedPages` frontmatter. Pages without `relatedPages` in their frontmatter do not display any related pages.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Limit" type="number" default="5">
  The maximum number of related pages to display. You can set this to any value between 1 and 10.
</ResponseField>

## Override related pages with frontmatter

Use the `relatedPages` frontmatter property to control related pages on individual pages. This works in both automatic and manual modes.

### Specify related pages manually

Set `relatedPages` to an array of internal paths or external URLs:

```yaml
---
title: "Authentication"
relatedPages:
  - /api-playground/overview
  - /deploy/authentication-setup
  - https://example.com/external-guide
---
```

### Set custom titles

Use the `Title: /path` syntax to provide a custom title for a related page link:

```yaml
---
title: "Authentication"
relatedPages:
  - Getting started with auth: /deploy/authentication-setup
  - API overview: /api-playground/overview
---
```

When you don't provide a custom title, Mintlify uses the page's title from your navigation. For external URLs without a custom title, the URL itself is displayed.

### Disable related pages on a page

Set `relatedPages` to `false` to hide related pages on a specific page:

```yaml
---
title: "Changelog"
relatedPages: false
---
```

<Note>
  Frontmatter-specified related pages are limited to a maximum of 10 entries, regardless of the limit set in the dashboard.
</Note>
