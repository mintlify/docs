## Usage

Use this endpoint to export unique visitor counts from your documentation. Each entry shows an approximate count of distinct visitors per page, split by human and AI bot traffic.

Paginate through results using `offset` and `limit`. Increment `offset` by `limit` while `hasMore` is `true`.

## Filtering

Filter visitors by date range using `dateFrom` and `dateTo` parameters.

## Response data

The response includes:
- **totals**: Site-wide unique visitor counts for the date range, split by `human`, `ai`, and `total` (deduplicated across traffic types)
- **visitors**: Per-path visitor counts, each with `human`, `ai`, and `total` fields
- **hasMore**: Whether additional pages of results exist

<Note>
  The `total` field is deduplicated across human and AI traffic. It represents the union of distinct visitor IDs with any qualifying content view, so it may be less than the sum of `human` and `ai`.
</Note>
