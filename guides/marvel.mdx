The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is the highest-grossing film franchise of all time, spanning interconnected films and television series based on Marvel Comics characters.

## The Infinity Saga

The first three phases of the MCU, building toward the ultimate showdown with Thanos.

### Phase one

<Steps>
  <Step title="Iron Man (2008)">
    Tony Stark builds a powered suit of armor and becomes Iron Man, launching the MCU.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Incredible Hulk (2008)">
    Bruce Banner searches for a cure to his gamma radiation condition while on the run from the military.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iron Man 2 (2010)">
    Tony Stark faces pressure from the government and a new enemy while dealing with his arc reactor's side effects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Thor (2011)">
    The Norse god of thunder is banished to Earth, where he learns humility and heroism.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)">
    Steve Rogers transforms from a frail young man into a super-soldier during World War II.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Avengers (2012)">
    Earth's mightiest heroes assemble for the first time to stop Loki and an alien invasion of New York.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The Infinity Stones

Six immensely powerful objects tied to different aspects of the universe:

| Stone | Color | Power |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Space | Blue | Teleportation and portal creation |
| Mind | Yellow | Telepathy and mental manipulation |
| Reality | Red | Altering the fabric of reality |
| Power | Purple | Immense energy and destructive force |
| Time | Green | Control over the flow of time |
| Soul | Orange | Dominion over living and dead souls |

<Warning>
  Wielding all six Infinity Stones simultaneously can be fatal, even for the most powerful beings in the universe.
</Warning>

## The Avengers

The Avengers are a team of extraordinary individuals brought together by Nick Fury and S.H.I.E.L.D. to fight threats no single hero could handle alone.

<Note>
  "I am Iron Man." — Tony Stark
</Note>

### Team roster highlights

- **Original six** — Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, Hawkeye
- **Phase two additions** — Scarlet Witch, Vision, Falcon, War Machine
- **Phase three additions** — Spider-Man, Black Panther, Doctor Strange, Captain Marvel

## The multiverse saga

After the Infinity Saga, the MCU expanded into the multiverse with Phase four and beyond:

- **WandaVision** — Wanda Maximoff's grief creates an alternate reality
- **Loki** — The god of mischief navigates the timeline and discovers the multiverse
- **Spider-Man: No Way Home** — The multiverse collides, bringing together three generations of Spider-Man
- **Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness** — Stephen Strange travels across realities
