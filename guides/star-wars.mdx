Star Wars is a legendary science fiction franchise that has captivated audiences for decades. From its groundbreaking films to its expanded universe, Star Wars continues to inspire fans worldwide.

## The galaxy far, far away

The Star Wars universe spans across multiple eras, featuring iconic characters, epic battles, and timeless stories of good versus evil.

### Key elements

- **The Force**: A mystical energy field that connects all living things
- **Jedi Order**: Peacekeepers who use the light side of the Force
- **Sith**: Dark side users who seek power and control
- **The Empire**: A totalitarian regime that rules the galaxy
- **The Rebellion**: Brave freedom fighters opposing the Empire

## Main saga

The Skywalker saga consists of three trilogies:

1. **Original trilogy** (Episodes IV-VI): Luke Skywalker's journey
2. **Prequel trilogy** (Episodes I-III): Anakin Skywalker's fall
3. **Sequel trilogy** (Episodes VII-IX): Rey's rise
