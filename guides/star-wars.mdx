Star Wars is a science fiction franchise created by George Lucas, beginning with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *Episode IV – A New Hope*). It has grown into one of the largest media franchises in history, spanning films, television series, novels, comics, and games.

## The galaxy

The story takes place "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away," following the conflict between the light and dark sides of the Force — a mystical energy field that binds the galaxy together.

## Core factions

- **The Jedi Order** — peacekeepers who draw on the light side of the Force.
- **The Sith** — practitioners of the dark side who seek power and domination.
- **The Galactic Empire** — authoritarian regime ruled by Emperor Palpatine.
- **The Rebel Alliance** — resistance fighters working to restore the Republic.
- **The First Order** and **The Resistance** — successors to the Empire and Rebellion in the sequel trilogy.

## Main saga

The "Skywalker Saga" spans nine episodic films released across three trilogies:

| Trilogy   | Episodes | Years     |
| --------- | -------- | --------- |
| Prequel   | I–III    | 1999–2005 |
| Original  | IV–VI    | 1977–1983 |
| Sequel    | VII–IX   | 2015–2019 |

## Notable characters

- **Luke Skywalker** — farm boy turned Jedi Knight.
- **Leia Organa** — princess, senator, and Rebel leader.
- **Han Solo** — smuggler and captain of the *Millennium Falcon*.
- **Darth Vader** — Sith Lord and central figure of the original trilogy.
- **Yoda** — ancient Jedi Master.
- **Rey** — scavenger from Jakku and central figure of the sequel trilogy.

## Beyond the films

The franchise has expanded through television series such as *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*, along with an extensive library of books, comics, and video games that explore the wider galaxy.
