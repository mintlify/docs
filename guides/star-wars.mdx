Star Wars is a multimedia franchise created by George Lucas that began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *Episode IV: A New Hope*). The saga has grown into one of the most influential entertainment franchises in history, spanning films, television series, books, comics, and video games.

## The original trilogy

The original trilogy introduced audiences to a galaxy far, far away and established the core mythology of the franchise.

## The prequel trilogy

The prequel trilogy explored the rise and fall of Anakin Skywalker and the fall of the Galactic Republic.

- **Episode I: The Phantom Menace** (1999) - The discovery of young Anakin Skywalker
- **Episode II: Attack of the Clones** (2002) - The beginning of the Clone Wars
- **Episode III: Revenge of the Sith** (2005) - Anakin's transformation into Darth Vader

## The sequel trilogy

The sequel trilogy continued the Skywalker saga with a new generation of heroes.

- **Episode VII: The Force Awakens** (2015)
- **Episode VIII: The Last Jedi** (2017)
- **Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker** (2019)

## Key characters

<Accordion title="Light side heroes">
  - **Luke Skywalker** - A farm boy who becomes a legendary Jedi
  - **Leia Organa** - Princess, rebel leader, and general
  - **Han Solo** - Smuggler turned hero
  - **Rey** - Scavenger who discovers her Force abilities
  - **Obi-Wan Kenobi** - Jedi Master who trained both Anakin and Luke
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Dark side villains">
  - **Darth Vader** - The fallen Jedi Anakin Skywalker
  - **Emperor Palpatine** - The Sith Lord behind the Galactic Empire
  - **Kylo Ren** - Ben Solo, torn between light and dark
  - **Darth Maul** - Sith apprentice with a double-bladed lightsaber
</Accordion>

## The Force

The Force is an energy field created by all living things. It binds the galaxy together and can be wielded by those sensitive to it.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Light side">
    The light side is aligned with compassion, selflessness, and peace. Jedi Knights use the light side to protect and serve others.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Dark side">
    The dark side draws power from fear, anger, and aggression. The Sith harness these emotions to dominate and control.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Cultural impact

Star Wars has had a profound influence on popular culture:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Revolutionized filmmaking">
    Industrial Light & Magic pioneered visual effects that transformed the film industry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Created a merchandise phenomenon">
    Action figures, toys, and collectibles became integral to the franchise's success.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expanded storytelling">
    The expanded universe of books, comics, and games created a rich mythology beyond the films.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspired generations">
    Themes of hope, redemption, and the battle between good and evil continue to resonate with audiences worldwide.
  </Step>
</Steps>
