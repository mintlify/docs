Star Wars is a science fantasy media franchise created by George Lucas. It began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *Episode IV: A New Hope*) and has expanded into one of the most recognizable franchises in the world.

## The saga

The core saga is divided into three trilogies, each focused on a generation of the Skywalker family.

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that binds the galaxy together. Its practitioners fall into two main traditions:

- **Jedi** — guardians of peace who follow the light side of the Force.
- **Sith** — adversaries who draw power from the dark side, fueled by passion and aggression.

## Key factions

| Faction | Era | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Galactic Republic | Prequels | Democratic government of the galaxy |
| Jedi Order | Prequels | Peacekeepers and Force users |
| Galactic Empire | Original trilogy | Authoritarian regime led by Emperor Palpatine |
| Rebel Alliance | Original trilogy | Resistance fighting to restore the Republic |
| First Order | Sequel trilogy | Successor to the Empire |
| Resistance | Sequel trilogy | Opposition to the First Order |

## Beyond the films

The franchise extends into television series (*The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, *Ahsoka*), animated shows (*The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*), novels, comics, and video games — collectively forming a vast continuity often referred to as the Star Wars universe.
