Bananas are one of the most popular fruits worldwide, known for their natural sweetness, convenience, and nutritional benefits.

## About bananas

Bananas grow on large herbaceous plants and are technically berries. They're cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

### Nutritional benefits

- Rich in potassium
- Good source of vitamin B6
- Contains dietary fiber
- Natural energy booster
- Supports heart health

## Types of bananas

### Cavendish

The most common variety found in grocery stores. Sweet flavor and creamy texture when ripe.

### Plantain

Larger and starchier than regular bananas. Often cooked before eating.

### Red bananas

Smaller with reddish-purple skin. Sweeter and creamier than yellow bananas.

### Lady finger

Also known as baby bananas. Sweeter and more delicate than Cavendish.

## How to enjoy bananas

- Fresh as a snack
- In smoothies
- Banana bread and baked goods
- Sliced on cereal or yogurt
- Frozen for a healthy dessert
- In pancakes or French toast

## Storage tips

Store bananas at room temperature until ripe. To slow ripening, refrigerate them (the peel may darken, but the fruit stays fresh). Separate bananas from the bunch to slow ripening further.
