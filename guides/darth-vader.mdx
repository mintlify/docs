Darth Vader is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. Originally Anakin Skywalker, a Jedi Knight, he turned to the dark side and became a powerful Sith Lord.

## Background

- **Birth name**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Homeworld**: Tatooine
- **Species**: Human
- **Affiliation**: Galactic Empire, Sith Order (formerly Jedi Order)

## Key characteristics

### The Force

Vader was one of the most powerful Force users in galactic history. His abilities included:

- Force choke
- Telekinesis
- Precognition
- Enhanced reflexes

### Combat skills

As both a Jedi and Sith, Vader mastered lightsaber combat, particularly Form V (Djem So).
