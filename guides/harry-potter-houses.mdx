Each major character in the Harry Potter series is sorted into one of the four Hogwarts houses — Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff — by the Sorting Hat upon arrival at school.

## House Assignments

| Character | House | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Harry Potter | Gryffindor | The Sorting Hat also considered Slytherin |
| Hermione Granger | Gryffindor | The Sorting Hat seriously considered Ravenclaw |
| Ron Weasley | Gryffindor |  |
| Albus Dumbledore | Gryffindor |  |
| Lord Voldemort | Slytherin | Originally sorted as Tom Riddle |
