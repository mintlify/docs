Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe. Standing at only 66 centimeters tall, this green-skinned, long-eared being served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order for centuries.

## Background

- **Species**: Unknown (referred to simply as "Yoda's species")
- **Height**: 66 cm
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Notable characteristics

### Wisdom and teaching

Yoda trained Jedi for over 800 years, making him one of the most experienced teachers in galactic history. His unique speech pattern, placing verbs at the end of sentences, became iconic:

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

### Combat abilities

Despite his small stature and advanced age, Yoda was a formidable warrior. His mastery of Form IV lightsaber combat (Ataru) allowed him to overcome larger opponents through speed and agility.

### Force abilities

Yoda possessed exceptional Force sensitivity, demonstrating mastery in:

- Telekinesis
- Force visions
- Mind tricks
- Force ghost manifestation

## Legacy

Yoda's influence on the Jedi Order and the galaxy cannot be overstated. Even after his physical death on Dagobah, he continued to guide new generations of Jedi as a Force ghost, appearing to Luke Skywalker and later to other Force-sensitive individuals.
