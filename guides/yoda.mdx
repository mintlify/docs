Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars galaxy. Standing at only 66 centimeters tall, this ancient being served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order for centuries.

## Background

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Lifespan**: ~900 years
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Notable traits

Yoda is known for his unique speech pattern, inverting typical sentence structure. His wisdom and mastery of the Force made him a respected leader and teacher.

<Note>
"Do or do not. There is no try." - Yoda
</Note>

## Force abilities

Yoda demonstrated exceptional Force abilities throughout his life:

- Telekinesis
- Force lightning absorption
- Enhanced agility despite his age
- Precognition and Force visions
- Lightsaber combat (Form IV: Ataru)

## Legacy

As one of the last remaining Jedi after Order 66, Yoda went into exile on the swamp planet Dagobah. There, he trained Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Force, passing on the Jedi teachings to a new generation.
