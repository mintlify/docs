Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe. Standing at only 66 centimeters tall, this green-skinned member of an unknown species lived for over 900 years and trained Jedi for over 800 of those years.

## Background

Yoda served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order and was a key member of the Jedi High Council during the final years of the Galactic Republic. His exceptional connection to the Force made him one of the most formidable Jedi in history.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Unknown (referred to simply as "Yoda's species")
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Lifespan**: Over 900 years
- **Lightsaber color**: Green
- **Notable ability**: Exceptional Force sensitivity and wisdom

## Famous quotes

<Note>
Yoda's distinctive speech pattern places the verb or object before the subject, making his wisdom both memorable and unique.
</Note>

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Luminous beings are we, not this crude matter."

## Legacy

Yoda's teachings continue to influence Jedi who came after him. His emphasis on patience, wisdom, and the light side of the Force shaped generations of Force users. Even after his physical death, Yoda has appeared as a Force ghost to guide those who follow the Jedi path.

<Info>
Fun fact: Yoda was originally going to be played by a monkey wearing a mask and carrying a cane, but this idea was scrapped in favor of a puppet designed by Stuart Freeborn.
</Info>
