Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars franchise. As a powerful Jedi Master and Grand Master of the Jedi Order, Yoda trained Jedi for over 800 years before the fall of the Galactic Republic.

## Background

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Key characteristics

Yoda is known for several distinctive traits:

- **Unique speech pattern**: Yoda speaks in an unusual syntax, often placing the verb at the end of sentences ("Strong with the Force, you are")
- **Exceptional Force abilities**: Despite his small stature, Yoda is one of the most powerful Force users in the galaxy
- **Wisdom**: His centuries of experience make him a source of profound insight and guidance
- **Lightsaber skills**: Master of Form IV (Ataru), known for acrobatic combat

## Legacy

Yoda's teachings continue to influence Jedi philosophy long after his passing. His emphasis on patience, mindfulness, and the dangers of attachment remain central to Jedi training.
