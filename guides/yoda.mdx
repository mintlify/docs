Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe. Standing at only 66 cm tall, this legendary figure trained Jedi for over 800 years.

## Background

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Lifespan**: Approximately 900 years
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Notable achievements

- Grand Master of the Jedi Order
- Trained generations of Jedi, including Luke Skywalker
- Member of the Jedi High Council
- One of the most powerful Force users in galactic history

## Famous quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Luminous beings are we, not this crude matter."

## Legacy

Yoda's teachings continue to influence Jedi philosophy and practice. His emphasis on patience, mindfulness, and the dangers of the dark side remain foundational to Jedi training.
