Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. As a legendary Jedi Master, he trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a crucial role in the galaxy's history.

## Background

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Key characteristics

Yoda is known for his distinctive speech pattern, reversing the typical sentence structure. His famous quotes include:

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

## Role in the saga

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prequel trilogy">
    Yoda serves as Grand Master of the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. He senses the growing darkness but cannot prevent the rise of the Sith.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Original trilogy">
    Living in exile on Dagobah, Yoda trains Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Force before becoming one with it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sequel trilogy">
    Yoda appears as a Force ghost to guide Luke, demonstrating that Jedi wisdom transcends physical form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
