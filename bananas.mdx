Bananas are one of the most popular fruits in the world. They're curved, yellow, and delicious.

## Why bananas are great

Bananas are packed with nutrients like potassium, vitamin B6, and vitamin C. They're also a great source of energy.

## Fun banana facts

- Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't
- The banana plant is actually an herb, not a tree
- There are over 1,000 different varieties of bananas
- Bananas float in water

## How to eat a banana

1. Peel the banana from the top or bottom
2. Remove the peel completely
3. Take a bite and enjoy

<Tip>
  Bananas pair perfectly with peanut butter for a healthy snack!
</Tip>

## Banana recipes

Try these delicious banana recipes:

- Banana bread
- Banana smoothies
- Chocolate-covered frozen bananas
- Banana pancakes
