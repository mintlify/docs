## Overview

The Fast and Furious franchise is one of the most successful action film series in cinema history. Starting in 2001, it has grown into a global phenomenon spanning multiple films, spin-offs, and a dedicated fanbase.

## The films

The main series includes the following films:

1. **The Fast and the Furious** (2001) - The original film that started it all, centered around illegal street racing in Los Angeles.
2. **2 Fast 2 Furious** (2003) - Brian O'Conner heads to Miami for a new undercover assignment.
3. **The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift** (2006) - A fresh story set in the underground drift racing world of Tokyo.
4. **Fast & Furious** (2009) - The original crew reunites for a high-stakes mission.
5. **Fast Five** (2011) - A heist in Rio de Janeiro that shifted the franchise toward action-heist territory.
6. **Fast & Furious 6** (2013) - The team is recruited to take down a skilled mercenary organization.
7. **Furious 7** (2015) - A mission of vengeance and a touching tribute to Paul Walker.
8. **The Fate of the Furious** (2017) - Dom is forced to betray his family.
9. **F9: The Fast Saga** (2021) - Dom faces his estranged brother Jakob.
10. **Fast X** (2023) - A new villain threatens everything the family has built.

## Key themes

- **Family** - The core message throughout the franchise: "I don't have friends. I got family."
- **Cars** - From muscle cars to supercars, vehicles are central to every story.
- **Loyalty** - Characters consistently put their bonds above personal gain.
- **Evolution** - The series evolved from street racing to global espionage and heists.
