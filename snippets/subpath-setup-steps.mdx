1. Navigate to the [Custom domain setup](https://app.mintlify.com/settings/deployment/custom-domain) page in your dashboard.
2. Select the **Subpath** hosting mode and enter your base path. For example, `/docs` or `/help`.
3. Choose the **Proxy only** traffic routing option, which is labeled with your base path, like **Proxy only /docs**.
4. Enter your domain and click **Add domain**.
