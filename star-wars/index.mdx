The Star Wars saga is one of the most beloved and influential film franchises in cinema history. From the original trilogy that captivated audiences in the late 1970s to the modern sequels, prequels, and standalone films, Star Wars has shaped popular culture for decades.

## The saga

The Star Wars films tell an epic story of good versus evil, set in a galaxy far, far away. The series follows the Skywalker family and their allies as they battle the dark forces of the Sith and the Empire.

## Original trilogy

The original trilogy introduced audiences to the world of Star Wars and remains a cultural touchstone:

- A new hope (1977)
- The empire strikes back (1980)
- Return of the Jedi (1983)

## Prequel trilogy

The prequel trilogy explored the fall of Anakin Skywalker and the rise of the Empire:

- The phantom menace (1999)
- Attack of the clones (2002)
- Revenge of the Sith (2005)

## Sequel trilogy

The sequel trilogy continued the story with a new generation of heroes:

- The force awakens (2015)
- The last Jedi (2017)
- The rise of Skywalker (2019)

## Standalone films

Additional films expanded the Star Wars universe:

- Rogue one (2016)
- Solo (2018)
