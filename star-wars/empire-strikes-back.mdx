**The empire strikes back** (1980) is widely considered one of the greatest sequels ever made. Directed by Irvin Kershner, this darker and more complex film deepened the Star Wars mythology and delivered one of cinema's most shocking plot twists.

## Plot

After the Rebels are brutally overpowered by the Empire on the ice planet Hoth, Luke Skywalker begins Jedi training with Yoda, while his friends are pursued by Darth Vader and bounty hunter Boba Fett all over the galaxy.

## Key moments

- The Battle of Hoth
- Luke's training with Yoda on Dagobah
- The reveal of Darth Vader's true identity
- Han Solo frozen in carbonite
- The Cloud City confrontation

## Characters introduced

- **Yoda**: The ancient and wise Jedi Master
- **Lando Calrissian**: Han Solo's old friend and administrator of Cloud City
- **Boba Fett**: The infamous bounty hunter
- **Emperor Palpatine**: The shadowy ruler of the Empire (first appearance)

## Critical acclaim

*The empire strikes back* is often ranked as the best Star Wars film and one of the greatest movies of all time. Its darker tone, character development, and unforgettable twist have made it a landmark in cinema history.
