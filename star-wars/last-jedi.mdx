**The last Jedi** (2017) takes the sequel trilogy in unexpected directions. Directed by Rian Johnson, the film challenges Star Wars traditions while exploring themes of failure, legacy, and finding your own path.

## Plot

Rey seeks out the legendary Luke Skywalker to learn the ways of the Force and help the Resistance fight the First Order. Meanwhile, the Resistance fleet is pursued by the First Order in a desperate chase across the galaxy.

## Key storylines

- Rey's training with the reluctant Luke Skywalker
- The Resistance's desperate escape from the First Order
- Finn and Rose's mission to disable the First Order's tracking system
- Kylo Ren's internal conflict and rise to power
- The connection between Rey and Kylo Ren

## New characters

- **Rose Tico**: A Resistance maintenance worker who joins Finn's mission
- **Vice Admiral Holdo**: A Resistance leader with an unexpected plan
- **DJ**: A morally ambiguous codebreaker

## Memorable moments

- The throne room battle
- Luke's Force projection confrontation with Kylo Ren
- The Battle of Crait
- Holdo's sacrifice (the Holdo maneuver)
- The stable boy with the broom

## Reception

*The last Jedi* was both critically acclaimed and divisive among fans, praised for its bold storytelling choices while criticized for subverting expectations.
