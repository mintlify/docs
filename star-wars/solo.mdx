**Solo** (2018) explores the early adventures of the galaxy's most famous smuggler. Directed by Ron Howard, the film shows how Han Solo became the character we meet in *A new hope*, including how he met Chewbacca and won the Millennium Falcon.

## Plot

Young Han Solo dreams of escaping his life of crime on Corellia and becoming a pilot. His adventures lead him to team up with a crew of smugglers, meet his future co-pilot Chewbacca, and cross paths with the smooth-talking Lando Calrissian.

## Key characters

- **Han Solo**: A young smuggler trying to make his way in the galaxy
- **Qi'ra**: Han's childhood friend and love interest
- **Tobias Beckett**: A seasoned criminal who becomes Han's mentor
- **Lando Calrissian**: The suave owner of the Millennium Falcon
- **L3-37**: Lando's outspoken droid companion
- **Chewbacca**: The Wookiee who becomes Han's lifelong friend

## Origin moments

The film reveals:
- How Han got his last name
- How he met Chewbacca and formed their friendship
- How he won the Millennium Falcon from Lando
- Han's Kessel Run in less than 12 parsecs
- His early relationship with Qi'ra

## Adventures

- The train heist on Vandor
- The Kessel Run
- The showdown on Savareen
- Meeting a familiar criminal organization

## Reception

While not as financially successful as other Star Wars films, *Solo* offers a fun adventure story that adds depth to one of the franchise's most beloved characters.
