**The rise of Skywalker** (2019) concludes the nine-film Skywalker saga. Directed by J.J. Abrams, the film brings together threads from all three trilogies for a final confrontation between good and evil.

## Plot

The Resistance faces its greatest challenge yet when Emperor Palpatine mysteriously returns from the dead. Rey, Finn, and Poe must embark on a dangerous mission to find Palpatine and stop the First Order once and for all, while Rey discovers shocking truths about her own identity.

## Major revelations

- Rey is revealed to be Palpatine's granddaughter
- The return of Emperor Palpatine
- The redemption of Ben Solo (Kylo Ren)
- Rey takes the Skywalker name

## Key moments

- The search for the Wayfinder
- The arrival of Lando and the citizen fleet
- Rey and Kylo's lightsaber duel on the Death Star wreckage
- Ben Solo's sacrifice
- Rey's final confrontation with Palpatine

## Legacy

The film attempts to bring closure to the nine-film saga, honoring the legacy of the Skywalker family while passing the torch to a new generation.

## Returning characters

- **Lando Calrissian**: Returns to aid the Resistance
- **Emperor Palpatine**: The ultimate villain returns
- **Force ghosts**: Luke, Leia, and other Jedi offer guidance
