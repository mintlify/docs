**Attack of the clones** (2002) continues the prequel trilogy, showing the galaxy descending into chaos as a separatist movement threatens the Republic. Directed by George Lucas, the film explores Anakin's growth as a Jedi and his forbidden romance with Padmé.

## Plot

Ten years after the events of *The phantom menace*, the galaxy is on the verge of civil war. As Obi-Wan Kenobi investigates an assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala, Anakin is assigned to protect her, leading to a forbidden romance that will have tragic consequences.

## Key developments

- Anakin and Padmé's secret romance
- The discovery of the clone army on Kamino
- The formation of the Separatist movement
- The beginning of the Clone Wars
- Anakin's first steps toward the dark side

## Important characters

- **Count Dooku**: A former Jedi turned Sith Lord
- **Jango Fett**: The template for the clone army and father of Boba Fett
- **Mace Windu**: A senior member of the Jedi Council
- **Yoda**: Returns to showcase his combat abilities

## Memorable sequences

- The chase through Coruscant
- The arena battle on Geonosis
- Yoda vs. Count Dooku lightsaber duel
- The reveal of the clone army
