**A new hope** (originally released as *Star Wars*) is the 1977 film that launched the Star Wars phenomenon. Directed by George Lucas, it introduced audiences to Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, and the iconic villain Darth Vader.

## Plot

A young farm boy named Luke Skywalker joins forces with a Jedi Knight, a cocky pilot, a Wookiee, and two droids to rescue a rebel princess and help destroy the evil Empire's devastating weapon, the Death Star.

## Key characters

- **Luke Skywalker**: A young farm boy who discovers his destiny as a Jedi
- **Princess Leia Organa**: A leader of the Rebel Alliance
- **Han Solo**: A smuggler with a heart of gold
- **Darth Vader**: The menacing Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi**: An aging Jedi Master
- **C-3PO and R2-D2**: The beloved droid duo

## Impact

*A new hope* revolutionized filmmaking with its groundbreaking special effects and created one of the most successful franchises in entertainment history. It won six Academy Awards and has been preserved in the National Film Registry.
