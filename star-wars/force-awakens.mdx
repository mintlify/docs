**The force awakens** (2015) launched the sequel trilogy and brought Star Wars back to theaters after a 10-year absence. Directed by J.J. Abrams, the film introduces new heroes while honoring the legacy of the original trilogy.

## Plot

Three decades after the defeat of the Empire, a new threat called the First Order has risen from its ashes. A scavenger named Rey, a defected stormtrooper named Finn, and resistance pilot Poe Dameron must team up with Han Solo and Chewbacca to find the missing Luke Skywalker and stop the First Order.

## New heroes

- **Rey**: A scavenger from Jakku with a mysterious connection to the Force
- **Finn**: A former stormtrooper who defects from the First Order
- **Poe Dameron**: A skilled Resistance pilot
- **BB-8**: An astromech droid carrying a vital map

## New villains

- **Kylo Ren**: The dark warrior and son of Han Solo and Leia Organa
- **Supreme Leader Snoke**: The mysterious leader of the First Order
- **General Hux**: The ruthless First Order military commander

## Legacy characters

The film brings back Han Solo, Leia Organa, Chewbacca, C-3PO, and R2-D2, while Luke Skywalker appears briefly at the end.

## Impact

*The force awakens* was a massive critical and commercial success, grossing over $2 billion worldwide and reigniting the Star Wars phenomenon for a new generation.
