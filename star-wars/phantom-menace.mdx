**The phantom menace** (1999) marked the return of Star Wars to theaters after 16 years. Directed by George Lucas, this prequel film takes us back to the origins of the saga, showing the discovery of young Anakin Skywalker and the first steps toward the fall of the Republic.

## Plot

Two Jedi Knights escape a hostile blockade to find allies and come across a young boy who may bring balance to the Force. However, the Sith resurface, threatening the peace of the galaxy.

## Reception

While *The phantom menace* was a box office success, it received mixed reviews from critics and fans. However, it introduced a new generation to Star Wars and laid the groundwork for the prequel trilogy's story.
