<Info>
  The agent is available on [Pro and Enterprise plans](https://mintlify.com/pricing?ref=agent).
</Info>

Connect your Linear workspace to the agent so you can assign documentation tasks as Linear issues. When you delegate an issue to the agent, it reads the issue context, updates your documentation, and opens a pull request.

## Connect your Linear workspace

1. Open the agent panel in your dashboard.
2. Click the **Settings** button.
3. In the Linear integration section, click **Install Linear app**.
4. Follow the Linear prompts to authorize the Mintlify app in your workspace.
5. After authorization, your dashboard shows the connected workspace name with a **Connected** status.

## Disconnect Linear

1. Open the agent panel in your dashboard.
2. Click the **Settings** button.
3. In the Linear integration section, click **Disconnect**.
4. Confirm the disconnection.

This removes the connection between your Linear workspace and the agent. You can reconnect at any time by following the setup steps again.
