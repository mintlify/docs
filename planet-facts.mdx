## Neptune

Three fun facts about Neptune:

- **Fastest winds in the solar system.** Neptune's atmosphere whips up supersonic winds that can exceed 1,500 miles per hour (2,400 km/h), faster than any other planet.
- **It was discovered by math, not by telescope.** Astronomers predicted Neptune's location using calculations based on irregularities in Uranus's orbit before it was ever observed, making it the first planet found through mathematical prediction in 1846.
- **A year on Neptune lasts 165 Earth years.** Since its discovery, Neptune has completed just one full orbit around the Sun (in 2011).
