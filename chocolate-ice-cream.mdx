Chocolate ice cream is a classic frozen dessert made with cocoa or chocolate, cream, and sugar. It's one of the most popular ice cream flavors worldwide.

## History

Chocolate ice cream has been enjoyed since the 18th century. It was one of the first ice cream flavors created after vanilla.

## Ingredients

The basic ingredients for chocolate ice cream include:

- Heavy cream
- Whole milk
- Sugar
- Cocoa powder or melted chocolate
- Egg yolks
- Vanilla extract

## Types of chocolate ice cream

### Dark chocolate

Made with dark cocoa or bittersweet chocolate, this variety has a rich, intense flavor.

### Milk chocolate

A sweeter, creamier version made with milk chocolate.

### Double chocolate

Features chocolate ice cream base with chocolate chips or chunks mixed in.

## Serving suggestions

Chocolate ice cream pairs well with:

- Hot fudge sauce
- Whipped cream
- Fresh berries
- Brownies
- Cookies

## Nutritional information

A typical serving (1/2 cup) contains:

- Calories: 140-160
- Fat: 7-9g
- Carbohydrates: 17-20g
- Protein: 2-3g
