Lovable is committed to delivering an exceptional customer experience. We provide technical support for paid projects running on our hosted Lovable platform, within the scope, hours, contacts, and channels outlined below.

## Scope

Lovable's technical support covers the following areas:

### Plans

- **Free plan**: Access to our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/lovable) and documentation
- **Starter, Launch & Scale plans**: Prioritized support through our in-product chat, powered by our AI assistant, with guaranteed access to human support when requested

### Configuration assistance

- Guidance on starting your project with Lovable
- Best practices for prompt engineering
- Using knowledge files effectively
- General questions about the Lovable stack and integrations
- Billing and account management support

### Troubleshooting

- Workarounds or resolutions for known issues
- Answering general "how-to" questions and pointing to relevant documentation
- Investigating and reporting bugs in Lovable's platform (UI, API, Supabase connection, etc.)
- Assistance with platform errors that persist after 2–3 attempts in chat/edit modes

### Not covered

Our technical support does not include:

- Debugging complex user application code beyond what AI or community support can handle
- Rewriting or adapting application code for compatibility with Lovable
- Modifying or patching unsupported third-party or open-source integrations
- Guidance with implementation of third-party or open-source integrations
- Project-specific debugging

## Who can contact support?

Support is available to customers using Lovable resources if:

1. The request is submitted via an official support channel (see below)
2. The request originates from a registered Lovable account email address
3. The account is part of a paying workspace (for direct support)

## Official support channels

### In-product support (preferred)

Paid users can submit support tickets directly inside the Lovable platform. Our AI assistant handles common queries instantly, and you can escalate to a human agent when needed.

To access in-product support:

1. Open your Lovable project
2. Click the support icon in the interface
3. Describe your issue or question
4. If the AI response doesn't resolve your issue, request to speak with a human agent

### Email support

If the in-product support form is unavailable, you can email support at [support@lovable.dev](mailto:support@lovable.dev).

After submitting your request, you'll receive a near-instant AI response to your inquiry. If that doesn't resolve your issue or answer your question, you can request to be routed to a human agent.

### Community support

All users, free and paying, can access our vibrant and helpful community in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/lovable) for community support.

Lovable team members and Community Champions may participate in community discussions, but official support is only guaranteed through the above-mentioned channels.

### Status page

For platform-wide issues, updates will be posted on our [status page](https://status.lovable.dev).

## Response times

Response times vary based on your plan and the severity of the issue:

- **AI assistant**: Instant response for all paid users
- **Human support**: Available during business hours for paid users
- **Community support**: Response times vary based on community availability

## Support best practices

To get the fastest and most effective support:

### Provide detailed information

Include in your support request:

- A clear description of the issue or question
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Screenshots or screen recordings when relevant
- Error messages (exact text)
- Your project URL or ID
- What you've already tried

### Check documentation first

Before contacting support, check our [documentation](https://docs.lovable.dev) for:

- Getting started guides
- Feature documentation
- Integration guides
- Troubleshooting tips
- Best practices

### Search the community

Many common questions have already been answered in our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/lovable). Use the search function to find existing discussions.

### Use the right channel

- **In-product support or email**: Account issues, billing, platform bugs, feature questions
- **Discord community**: General questions, project ideas, community feedback, learning from others
- **Status page**: Platform-wide outages or incidents

## Escalation

If you're not satisfied with the support you've received:

1. Request to speak with a supervisor or senior support agent
2. Provide your ticket number and a summary of the issue
3. Explain what resolution you're seeking

For urgent issues affecting production applications, mark your support request as "urgent" and include details about the business impact.

## Feedback

We continuously improve our support based on your feedback. After your support interaction, you may receive a survey asking about your experience. We appreciate you taking the time to share your thoughts.
