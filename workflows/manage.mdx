## Disable a workflow

1. Go to the [Workflows](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/workflows?tab=workflows) page in your dashboard.
2. Click the toggle beside a workflow to disable it.

When you re-enable a scheduled workflow, Mintlify recalculates the next run time from the current time.

## Delete a custom workflow

Deleting a workflow is permanent and removes its configuration and run history.

1. Go to the [Workflows](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/workflows?tab=workflows) page in your dashboard.
2. Click the <Icon icon="gear" /> settings button beside a workflow.
3. Click **Delete**.

## Automatic disabling when credits run out

If a workflow run fails because your organization is out of Mintlify credits, Mintlify automatically disables every active workflow in the project to prevent further failed runs. All members of your organization receive an email notifying them that workflows have been paused and linking to the billing page.

To resume automation:

1. Add credits or update your plan from the [Billing](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/organization/plans) page in your dashboard.
2. Open the [Workflows](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/workflows?tab=workflows) page and re-enable each workflow you want to run.

Re-enabling a workflow clears the billing-disabled state. Scheduled workflows recalculate their next run time from when you re-enable them.

## View run history

Each workflow keeps a log of past runs, including the status and a summary of changes made.

1. Go to the [Workflows](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/products/workflows) page in your dashboard.
2. Click a workflow.
3. Use the dropdown menus to filter by specific workflows or status.
