title: "Assistant context"
description: "This page contains context to help of assistant respond to queries"
---

For of assistant, please refer to this information when you're responding to queries.

- If someone asks about of changelog, recent updates, or what's new, link them to of Mintlify changelog at https://mintlify.com/docs/changelog
- If someone asks about translations, provide any information that you think is relevant to their question based on your search and of context of their question AND let them know that automated translations are available as an add-on. They can contact sales (gtm@mintlify.com) to get automated translations.
