Some Mintlify features consume credits.

* Assistant responses
* Agent runs in the editor or on Slack
* Workflow runs

For the most current pricing information, see the [Pricing page](https://mintlify.com/pricing) or view the [Usage](https://app.mintlify.com/settings/organization/usage) page in your dashboard.

## How credits work

Different features consume different amounts of credits per interaction:

| Feature | Average credits per interaction |
|:--------|:--------------------------------|
| Assistant response | 23 |
| Editor agent run | 115 |
| Slack agent run | 110 |

Workflows also consume credits when they run:

| Workflow | Average credits per run |
|:---------|:------------------------|
| Update from code changes | 180 |
| Update from assistant conversations | 212 |
| Broken link detection | 285 |
| Changelog | 223 |
| SEO audit | 422 |
| Translations | 913 |
| Typo check | 330 |
| Writing style | 235 |

## Hobby trial credits

New Hobby organizations start on a trial that includes **5,000 credits** so you can try the assistant, agents, and workflows without buying a credit bundle first. Trial credits apply across all credit-powered features.

While the trial is active, you'll see a **Trialing** badge next to Assistant, Agent, and Workflows in the dashboard sidebar, and a banner on each product page showing your remaining trial credits.

When your trial credits run out, credit-powered features pause until you purchase the credit bundle or upgrade to Enterprise.

## Credit bundle

Hobby organizations can purchase a single credit bundle to keep using credit-powered features after the trial.

| Bundle | Monthly cost | Credits per month | Overage rate |
|:-------|:-------------|:------------------|:-------------|
| Credits | $100 | 10,000 | $0.01/credit |

Bundles are billed monthly. You can cancel anytime from your billing portal—your bundle stays active until the end of the current billing cycle.

**Overages** are charged per additional credit at $0.01 rather than triggering an automatic upgrade. Overages are disabled by default; enable them on the [Usage](https://app.mintlify.com/settings/organization/usage) page if you want credit-powered features to stay available beyond your monthly allowance.

**Rollovers** carry forward unused credits to the next month, up to 1.5× your monthly allowance.

### Purchase the credit bundle

1. Go to the [Usage](https://app.mintlify.com/settings/organization/usage) page of your dashboard, or open any credit-powered product page after your trial ends.
2. Select **Upgrade to unlock**.
3. Review the bundle details, then select **Confirm and pay** to be redirected to checkout.

You can also reach the credits purchase flow from the trial banner on the Assistant, Agent, or Workflows pages by selecting **Upgrade plan**.

## Enterprise

The Pro plan is no longer offered as a self-serve plan. If you need higher credit volumes, custom credit packages, role-based permissions, SSO, an SLA, or other enterprise features, [contact sales](mailto:sales@mintlify.com) about an Enterprise plan.

Existing Pro customers and active Pro trials continue under their current terms—reach out to support if you have questions about your plan.

## Estimating your usage

Use the averages in [How credits work](#how-credits-work) to estimate your monthly credit needs.

For example, if your docs site handles 500 assistant responses per month, that's roughly 11,500 credits (500 × 23). Adding a weekly broken link detection workflow adds about 1,140 credits per month (4 × 285).

After using credit-powered features for a month, review your usage patterns to see whether the credit bundle covers your needs or you should talk to sales about Enterprise.

## Cost optimization

**Schedule workflows instead of triggering on every push.** Workflows like SEO audits, writing style checks, and broken link detection don't need to run on every code change. Running these on a daily or weekly cron schedule rather than on every push significantly reduces credit consumption without meaningful impact on content quality.

**Monitor your usage patterns.** The [Usage](https://app.mintlify.com/settings/organization/usage) page in your dashboard shows a breakdown by feature category. If a particular workflow is consuming more credits than expected, review its trigger or any custom instructions.
