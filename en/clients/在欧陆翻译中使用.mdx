- Click the gear icon in the bottom left corner to enter the settings page, then select "Translation Engine" and "Download More Translation Engines."  
![Image](../media/oulu1.png)  
- Add a translation engine and select our AiHubMix.  
![Image](../media/oulu2.png)  
- Enter the [Key from our site](https://aihubmix.com/token) in the "API Key" field.  
- Select the model and click to authenticate. Once verified, click OK.  
![Image](../media/oulu3.png)
