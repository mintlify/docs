## Fun fact

Octopuses have three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, while the third circulates it to the rest of the body. Curiously, the main heart stops beating when they swim, which is why they prefer crawling.

Penguins propose to their mates with a pebble. A male will search the beach for the smoothest, most perfect stone and present it to the female he wants to partner with — if she accepts, the pebble becomes part of their nest.
