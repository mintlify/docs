---
title: "Go-live checklist"
description: "A checklist to verify your Mintlify site is ready before switching your production domain."
---

Use this checklist to confirm your Mintlify site is fully configured before you point production traffic to it. Work through each section in order, then flip your DNS once every item is complete.

## Content

<Checkbox>All pages render without build errors</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Navigation matches your intended information architecture</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Internal links resolve to the correct pages</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Images, videos, and other assets load correctly</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Code blocks display with proper syntax highlighting</Checkbox> <Checkbox>OpenAPI specs render and the API playground works</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Redirects from your old URLs to new paths are configured in `docs.json`</Checkbox>

## Branding and appearance

<Checkbox>Logo, favicon, and theme colors match your brand</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Custom fonts load correctly in light and dark mode</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Navbar links, footer, and social links are configured</Checkbox> <Checkbox>404 page is customized</Checkbox>

## Authentication and access

<Checkbox>Dashboard SSO is configured with your identity provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, etc.). See [Single sign-on (SSO)](/dashboard/sso).</Checkbox> <Checkbox>SCIM user provisioning is enabled if you manage members through your IdP. See [SCIM user provisioning](/dashboard/scim).</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Roles are assigned (admin, editor, viewer) for every team member. See [Roles](/dashboard/roles).</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Dashboard access policies (IP allowlist, session lifetime) are set. See [Access policies](/dashboard/network-access).</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Documentation authentication is configured if your docs are private. See [Authentication setup](/deploy/authentication-setup).</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Personalization rules are tested for authenticated readers</Checkbox>

## Domain and hosting

<Checkbox>Custom domain is added in the dashboard</Checkbox> <Checkbox>DNS `TXT` verification records are added and verified</Checkbox> <Checkbox>TLS certificate is provisioned</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Canonical URL is set in `docs.json`</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Subpath or reverse proxy setup is tested (if applicable)</Checkbox>

## Search and AI

<Checkbox>In-product search returns relevant results</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Assistant answers questions accurately with citations</Checkbox> <Checkbox>`llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt` are generated</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Contextual menu is enabled with your preferred AI integrations</Checkbox>

## Analytics and integrations

<Checkbox>Analytics platform is connected (GA4, PostHog, Segment, etc.)</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Support widget (Intercom, Front) is configured if applicable</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Cookie consent or privacy integration is enabled if required</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Feedback widget is enabled</Checkbox>

## Deployment

<Checkbox>Git integration (GitHub, GitLab) is connected to the correct branch</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Preview deployments are working on pull requests</Checkbox> <Checkbox>CI checks (broken links, Vale, `mint broken-links`) pass</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Audit logs are being recorded</Checkbox>

## Final cutover

<Checkbox>Announce the launch to your team</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Point production DNS `CNAME` to `cname.mintlify.builders`</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Verify HTTPS works on the production domain</Checkbox> <Checkbox>Spot-check top pages after DNS propagates</Checkbox>
