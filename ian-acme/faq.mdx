# Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about Ian ACME.

## Getting started

### How do I create an account?

Visit [ian-acme.mintlify.app](https://ian-acme.mintlify.app) and click **Sign up**. Enter your email and create a password. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan. No credit card required.

### What browsers are supported?

Ian ACME works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

## Account and billing

### How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to **Settings** > **Billing** and click **Upgrade Plan**. Choose your desired tier and enter payment information.

### Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can request invoice billing.

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable.

## Features and functionality

### How many team members can I add?

- **Free** - Up to 5 members
- **Pro** - Up to 50 members
- **Enterprise** - Unlimited members

### Can I integrate with other tools?

Yes! Ian ACME integrates with popular tools including Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, and more. See our [integrations page](/ian-acme/integrations) for the full list.

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes, Ian ACME is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export projects, tasks, and other data at any time in CSV or JSON format. Go to **Settings** > **Export Data**.

## Security and privacy

### How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard security measures:
- 256-bit SSL encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II certified

### Where is data stored?

Data is stored in secure, redundant data centers in the United States and Europe. Enterprise customers can choose their preferred region.

### Is two-factor authentication available?

Yes, we highly recommend enabling 2FA. Set it up in **Settings** > **Security**.

### Who can see my data?

Only users you explicitly invite to your workspace can access your data. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password

Click **Forgot Password** on the login page. Enter your email and we'll send reset instructions.

### I'm not receiving notifications

Check:
1. Your notification settings in **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Your email spam/junk folder
3. That your email address is verified

### Tasks aren't syncing

Try these steps:
1. Refresh your browser
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Log out and log back in

### The app is slow or unresponsive

Performance tips:
- Close unused browser tabs
- Archive old projects
- Reduce the number of tasks displayed
- Try a different browser

## Technical questions

### Does Ian ACME have an API?

Yes! Check our [API documentation](/ian-acme/api) for details on integrating with Ian ACME programmatically.

### Can I use Ian ACME offline?

The mobile apps offer limited offline functionality. Changes sync automatically when you're back online.

### What are the system requirements?

Minimum requirements:
- Modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
- Internet connection
- 4GB RAM (8GB recommended)

### Can I self-host Ian ACME?

Self-hosting is available for Enterprise customers. Contact sales@ian-acme.com for details.
