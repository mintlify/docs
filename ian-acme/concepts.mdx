# Core concepts

Learn about the key concepts that power the Ian ACME platform.

## Workspaces

A workspace is your organization's home in Ian ACME. It contains all your projects, team members, and settings.

**Key features:**
- Centralized team management
- Shared resources and integrations
- Customizable permissions
- Usage analytics

## Projects

Projects are the primary way to organize work in Ian ACME. Each project represents a specific initiative or goal.

**Project components:**
- Tasks and subtasks
- Milestones and deadlines
- Team assignments
- Files and documentation

## Tasks

Tasks are individual units of work within a project. They help break down complex projects into manageable pieces.

**Task properties:**
- Title and description
- Assignees
- Due dates
- Priority levels
- Status tracking
- Comments and attachments

## Teams

Teams are groups of users who collaborate within a workspace. You can create multiple teams for different departments or projects.

**Team features:**
- Role-based permissions
- Member management
- Activity tracking
- Team-specific settings

## Permissions

Ian ACME uses a role-based permission system to control access:

- **Admin** - Full access to workspace settings and all projects
- **Member** - Can create and manage projects they're assigned to
- **Guest** - Limited access to specific projects only
- **Viewer** - Read-only access to assigned projects

## Integrations

Connect Ian ACME with your existing tools to streamline workflows:

- **Communication** - Slack, Microsoft Teams
- **Development** - GitHub, GitLab, Jira
- **Storage** - Google Drive, Dropbox
- **Calendar** - Google Calendar, Outlook
