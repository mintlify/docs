# Quickstart guide

Get up and running with Ian ACME in just a few simple steps.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- An Ian ACME account (sign up at [ian-acme.mintlify.app](https://ian-acme.mintlify.app))
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

## Step 1: Create your account

1. Navigate to [ian-acme.mintlify.app](https://ian-acme.mintlify.app)
2. Click **Sign up** in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you

## Step 2: Set up your workspace

Once logged in, you'll be prompted to create your first workspace:

1. Enter a workspace name (e.g., "My Company")
2. Choose your workspace settings
3. Invite team members (optional)
4. Click **Create workspace**

## Step 3: Create your first project

Now you're ready to create a project:

1. From your workspace dashboard, click **New Project**
2. Enter a project name and description
3. Set project permissions and visibility
4. Click **Create**

## Step 4: Explore the platform

Take a tour of Ian ACME's key features:

- **Dashboard** - View all your projects and activity
- **Projects** - Organize work into manageable units
- **Tasks** - Break down projects into actionable items
- **Team** - Manage members and permissions
- **Settings** - Customize your workspace
