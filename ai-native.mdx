Mintlify's built-in AI features help users find answers and your team maintain content—using your documentation as context.

## What makes your documentation AI-native

- **Reading:** Users chat with the [assistant](/ai/assistant) for instant answers, or embed it via the [API](/api/assistant/create-assistant-message-v2).
- **Writing:** The [agent](/agent) opens PRs from prompts, PRs, and Slack threads—via Slack, [API](/api/agent/v2/create-agent-job), or [workflows](/agent/workflows). Configure [Cursor](/guides/cursor), [Claude Code](/guides/claude-code), and [Windsurf](/guides/windsurf) to follow your schema and style guide.
- **Discovering:** Pages serve Markdown (append `.md` to any URL), hosted `llms.txt`, `skill.md`, and an MCP server expose content to LLMs, and semantic search plus 404 suggestions work out of the box.
