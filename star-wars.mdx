## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas, first released in 1977. It has grown into one of the most popular and influential media franchises of all time.

## The saga

The main Skywalker saga spans nine films across three trilogies:

- **Original trilogy** (1977-1983): A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back, Return of the Jedi
- **Prequel trilogy** (1999-2005): The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, Revenge of the Sith
- **Sequel trilogy** (2015-2019): The Force Awakens, The Last Jedi, The Rise of Skywalker

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that binds the galaxy together. Practitioners are divided into:

- **Jedi**: Guardians of peace who follow the light side
- **Sith**: Dark side users who seek power and domination
