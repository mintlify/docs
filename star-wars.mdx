## The saga

Star Wars is a space opera franchise created by George Lucas. The story follows the battle between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, intertwined with the journey of the Skywalker family and the power of the Force.

## The original trilogy

- **Episode IV: A New Hope** (1977) — Luke Skywalker joins the Rebel Alliance to rescue Princess Leia and destroy the Death Star.
- **Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back** (1980) — The Empire retaliates, and Luke discovers a shocking truth about Darth Vader.
- **Episode VI: Return of the Jedi** (1983) — The Rebels launch a final assault on the second Death Star, and Luke confronts the Emperor.

## The prequel trilogy

- **Episode I: The Phantom Menace** (1999) — Jedi discover young Anakin Skywalker and face the return of the Sith.
- **Episode II: Attack of the Clones** (2002) — The Clone Wars begin as Anakin's path toward the dark side deepens.
- **Episode III: Revenge of the Sith** (2005) — Anakin falls to the dark side and becomes Darth Vader.

## The sequel trilogy

- **Episode VII: The Force Awakens** (2015) — A new hero, Rey, emerges as the First Order threatens the galaxy.
- **Episode VIII: The Last Jedi** (2017) — Rey seeks out Luke Skywalker while the Resistance fights for survival.
- **Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker** (2019) — The final battle between the Resistance and the resurrected Emperor Palpatine.
