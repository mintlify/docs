## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas. The saga follows the struggle between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, set in a galaxy far, far away.

## The films

### Original trilogy (1977-1983)

| Episode | Title | Year |
|---------|-------|------|
| IV | A New Hope | 1977 |
| V | The Empire Strikes Back | 1980 |
| VI | Return of the Jedi | 1983 |

The original trilogy introduces Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, and Darth Vader. It follows Luke's journey from farm boy to Jedi Knight as the Rebellion fights to overthrow the Empire.

### Prequel trilogy (1999-2005)

| Episode | Title | Year |
|---------|-------|------|
| I | The Phantom Menace | 1999 |
| II | Attack of the Clones | 2002 |
| III | Revenge of the Sith | 2005 |

The prequel trilogy explores Anakin Skywalker's transformation into Darth Vader and the fall of the Galactic Republic.

### Sequel trilogy (2015-2019)

| Episode | Title | Year |
|---------|-------|------|
| VII | The Force Awakens | 2015 |
| VIII | The Last Jedi | 2017 |
| IX | The Rise of Skywalker | 2019 |

The sequel trilogy follows a new generation of heroes—Rey, Finn, and Poe—as they face the First Order and the return of the Sith.

## Timeline

The Skywalker Saga spans nine episodic films:

1. **Episodes I-III** - The fall of the Republic and rise of the Empire
2. **Episodes IV-VI** - The Galactic Civil War and redemption of Anakin Skywalker
3. **Episodes VII-IX** - The conflict between the Resistance and First Order

## Standalone films

- **Rogue One** (2016) - The mission to steal the Death Star plans
- **Solo** (2018) - Han Solo's origin story
