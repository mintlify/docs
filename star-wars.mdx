Star Wars is a legendary science fiction franchise created by George Lucas in 1977. It has become one of the most influential media franchises in history.

## The original trilogy

The original trilogy introduced audiences to a galaxy far, far away:

- **Episode IV: A New Hope** (1977) - Luke Skywalker joins the Rebel Alliance
- **Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back** (1980) - The rebels face the Empire's wrath
- **Episode VI: Return of the Jedi** (1983) - The final battle against the Empire

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that connects all living things. Users of the Force are divided into:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Jedi">
    The Jedi are peacekeepers who use the light side of the Force. They value:
    - Peace and harmony
    - Knowledge and defense
    - Self-discipline
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sith">
    The Sith embrace the dark side of the Force. They are driven by:
    - Power and ambition
    - Passion and emotion
    - Domination
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
