## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas. The saga has captivated audiences since 1977 with its compelling story of good versus evil, set against the backdrop of a galaxy filled with diverse planets, alien species, and advanced technology.

## The original trilogy

The journey began with three groundbreaking films:

- **A New Hope** (1977) - Luke Skywalker discovers his destiny as a Jedi
- **The Empire Strikes Back** (1980) - The dark revelation that changed everything
- **Return of the Jedi** (1983) - The final confrontation between the Empire and the Rebellion

## Key concepts

### The Force

The Force is a mystical energy field that connects all living things in the galaxy. Those sensitive to the Force can harness its power for extraordinary abilities.

### Jedi and Sith

- **Jedi** - Peacekeepers who follow the light side of the Force
- **Sith** - Warriors who embrace the dark side seeking power and domination

### Iconic characters

The saga features memorable characters including Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, Darth Vader, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Yoda, each playing a crucial role in the battle between light and darkness.

## The expanded universe

Beyond the films, Star Wars has grown to include television series, books, comics, and games, creating one of the most expansive fictional universes in entertainment history.
