Star Wars is a multimedia franchise created by George Lucas that began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *Episode IV: A New Hope*). It has since expanded into one of the most influential media franchises in entertainment history.

## Timeline

The Star Wars saga spans thousands of years of fictional history, with the main stories divided into three trilogies:

- **Prequel Trilogy** (Episodes I-III): Chronicles the fall of the Galactic Republic and Anakin Skywalker's transformation into Darth Vader
- **Original Trilogy** (Episodes IV-VI): Follows Luke Skywalker and the Rebel Alliance's fight against the Galactic Empire
- **Sequel Trilogy** (Episodes VII-IX): Continues the story with a new generation of heroes facing the First Order

## Expanded universe

Beyond the films, Star Wars includes:

- **Television series**: The Clone Wars, The Mandalorian, Andor, Ahsoka
- **Books and comics**: Thousands of stories expanding the lore
- **Video games**: Knights of the Old Republic, Jedi: Fallen Order, Battlefront
- **Theme park attractions**: Galaxy's Edge at Disney parks

## Cultural impact

Star Wars revolutionized filmmaking with its groundbreaking visual effects, memorable score by John Williams, and innovative merchandising. The phrase "May the Force be with you" has become part of global popular culture.
