## A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...

This page is a lighthearted, Star Wars-themed reference added to the docs.

## Factions

- **Jedi Order** — Guardians of peace and justice, wielders of the light side of the Force.
- **Sith** — Practitioners of the dark side, driven by passion and power.
- **Rebel Alliance** — Resistance fighters opposing the Galactic Empire.
- **Galactic Empire** — Authoritarian regime led by Emperor Palpatine.
