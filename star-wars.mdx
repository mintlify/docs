Star Wars is a space-opera franchise created by George Lucas, beginning with the 1977 film later subtitled _A New Hope_. What started as a single film grew into a saga spanning nine mainline episodes, standalone films, and a deep catalog of series, novels, and games.

## The Skywalker Saga

The core nine-film narrative is grouped into three trilogies, each following a generation of the Skywalker family.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Original Trilogy" icon="star">
    Episodes IV–VI (1977–1983). Luke Skywalker's rise, the Rebellion against the Empire, and the redemption of Darth Vader.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Prequel Trilogy" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Episodes I–III (1999–2005). Anakin Skywalker's fall to the dark side and the collapse of the Republic.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Sequel Trilogy" icon="forward">
    Episodes VII–IX (2015–2019). A new generation faces the First Order as old heroes return.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that binds the galaxy together. Those sensitive to it can train to sense and shape it.

<Tip>
  The two best-known traditions are the **Jedi**, who draw on the light side through peace and discipline, and the **Sith**, who channel the dark side through passion and power.
</Tip>

## Recommended watch order

There's no single "correct" order, but release order is the most common recommendation for first-time viewers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Original Trilogy">
    Start with Episodes IV, V, and VI to experience the story as audiences first did — including the franchise's most famous twist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prequel Trilogy">
    Continue with Episodes I–III for the backstory of Anakin Skywalker and the origins of the Empire.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sequel Trilogy">
    Finish with Episodes VII–IX to close out the Skywalker Saga.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Beyond the films

The galaxy extends well past the mainline episodes — standalone films like _Rogue One_ and _Solo_, animated series such as _The Clone Wars_ and _Rebels_, and live-action series including _The Mandalorian_ and _Andor_.
