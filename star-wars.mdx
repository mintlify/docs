## Scene: The duel on Cloud City

<Note>
  Bespin. The carbon-freezing chamber. Steam hisses from the floor grates. A pale blue glow spills across the walls as two lightsabers ignite.
</Note>

### Setting

- **Location:** Carbon-freezing chamber, Cloud City, Bespin
- **Time:** Shortly after the Battle of Hoth
- **Atmosphere:** Cold, metallic, lit by flashes of red and blue

### Characters present

| Character | Weapon | Intent |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Luke Skywalker | Blue lightsaber | Rescue his friends |
| Darth Vader | Red lightsaber | Capture Luke |

### The exchange

Vader advances through the mist, cape trailing behind him. Luke raises his saber, breath ragged. Sparks fly as their blades meet. Vader forces Luke back toward the central shaft, then severs Luke's right hand in a single stroke. The lightsaber tumbles into the abyss below.

Luke clings to a narrow gantry above the reactor shaft, cradling the stump of his wrist.

> **Vader:** "Obi-Wan never told you what happened to your father."
>
> **Luke:** "He told me enough! He told me you killed him!"
>
> **Vader:** "No. *I* am your father."
>
> **Luke:** "No. No. That's not true. That's impossible!"

Wind howls through the open shaft. Luke stares into the void, then lets go — falling into the clouds of Bespin, leaving Vader alone above.

### Why the scene matters

- Reframes the saga's central conflict as a family tragedy.
- Marks Luke's first real defeat and the loss of his father's lightsaber.
- Sets up the redemption arc that completes in *Return of the Jedi*.
