Star Wars is an epic space-opera franchise created by George Lucas, beginning with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *A New Hope*). It has grown into one of the most influential and recognizable media franchises in history, spanning films, television series, novels, comics, and games.

## The galaxy

The saga takes place "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away" and follows the conflict between the light and dark sides of the Force — a mystical energy field that binds the galaxy together.

## Key factions

- **The Jedi Order** — guardians of peace who draw on the light side of the Force.
- **The Sith** — dark-side adversaries who seek power and domination.
- **The Rebel Alliance / Resistance** — freedom fighters opposing tyrannical regimes.
- **The Galactic Empire / First Order** — authoritarian powers seeking galactic control.

## Iconic characters

- Luke Skywalker
- Leia Organa
- Han Solo
- Darth Vader
- Yoda
- Obi-Wan Kenobi
- Rey
- Kylo Ren

## The Skywalker Saga

The main film series consists of three trilogies:

1. **Prequel trilogy** (Episodes I–III)
2. **Original trilogy** (Episodes IV–VI)
3. **Sequel trilogy** (Episodes VII–IX)

## Beyond the films

The franchise has expanded into acclaimed television series such as *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*, along with animated shows, novels, and video games that deepen the lore of the galaxy.
