After you complete this guide, you'll have a live documentation site ready to customize and update.

## Deploy your documentation site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect GitHub (optional)">
    Connect your GitHub account to use your own repository, or skip to use a Mintlify-hosted repository.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Follow the onboarding prompts to set up your documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your site is deployed">
    Your documentation is deployed at your `.mintlify.app` URL. Find it on your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with a Mintlify-hosted repository for quick setup. You can migrate to your own repository anytime.
</Tip>

## Make your first change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the web editor">
    Navigate to the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor) in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Open `index.mdx` and update the description in the frontmatter:
    
    ```mdx
    ---
    title: "Introduction"
    description: "Your custom description here"
    ---
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click the **Publish** button in the top-right of the web editor toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View live">
    Refresh your documentation site to see your changes live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
