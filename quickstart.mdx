## Deploy your site

[Create an account](https://mintlify.com/start) and complete onboarding. Your site deploys automatically to `https://<your-project-name>.mintlify.app`.

## Make your first edit

1. Open the [Editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com) in your dashboard
2. Select `index.mdx` and update the title
3. Click **Publish**
