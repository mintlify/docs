## Deploy your site

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mintlify-hosted">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your account">
        Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) and create your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Skip GitHub connection">
        Click **Skip** when prompted to connect GitHub.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete onboarding">
        Follow the prompts. Mintlify creates a private repository and deploys your site automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Your own repository">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your account">
        Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) and create your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect GitHub">
        Log in to GitHub and authenticate when prompted.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the GitHub App">
        Install the [GitHub App](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/api/github/install-app) to enable automatic deployments.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Your site is deployed at `https://<your-project-name>.mintlify.app`. Find your URL on the [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).

## Make your first change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the web editor">
    Navigate to the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Open `index.mdx` and update the description:
    
    ```mdx
    ---
    title: "Introduction"
    description: "Your custom description here"
    ---
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the toolbar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
