Get your documentation site live in minutes and make your first edit.

## Deploy your site

1. Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start)
2. Connect your GitHub account
3. Create or select a repository for your documentation
4. Complete the onboarding process

Your site is now live at `https://<your-project-name>.mintlify.app`.

## Find your site URL

Your exact URL is on the **Overview** page of your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).

## Make your first edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the web editor">
    Go to the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor) in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Open `index.mdx` and change the description:

    ```mdx
    ---
    title: "Introduction"
    description: "Your custom description here"
    ---
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your changes">
    Refresh your documentation site to see your changes live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Prefer working locally?** Install the [CLI](/installation) to edit files in your preferred editor and preview changes with `mint dev`.
</Tip>
