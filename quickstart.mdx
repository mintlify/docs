After you complete this guide, you'll have a live documentation site ready to customize and update.

## Deploy your documentation site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your repository">
    Use a **Mintlify-hosted** repository for quick setup, or connect **your own repository** for local development. You can migrate between options anytime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete setup">
    Follow the onboarding prompts. If using your own repository, install the [GitHub App](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/api/github/install-app) to enable automatic deployments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your site is deployed">
    Your documentation is deployed at your `.mintlify.app` URL. Find it on your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make your first change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the web editor">
    Navigate to the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor) in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Open `index.mdx` and update the description:
    
    ```mdx
    ---
    title: "Introduction"
    description: "Your custom description here"
    ---
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the top-right toolbar, then refresh your site to see changes live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
