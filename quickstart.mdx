## Deploy your site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect GitHub (optional)">
    Connect your GitHub account to use your own repository, or click **Skip** for a Mintlify-hosted repository.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete setup">
    Follow the onboarding prompts. Your site deploys automatically at your `.mintlify.app` URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Find your URL on the **Overview** page of your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).

## Make your first change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the web editor">
    Navigate to the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Open `index.mdx` and update the description:
    
    ```mdx
    ---
    description: "Your custom description here"
    ---
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the toolbar. Your changes deploy automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
