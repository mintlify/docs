## Deploy your documentation site

Go to [mintlify.com/start](https://mintlify.com/start) to deploy your site. You'll connect your GitHub account and create or select a repository for your documentation.

After onboarding, your site is live at `https://<your-project-name>.mintlify.app`. Find your URL on the **Overview** page of your [dashboard](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/).

## Make your first change

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web editor">
    1. Open the [web editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor) in your dashboard
    2. Edit `index.mdx` and update the description
    3. Click **Publish** to deploy your changes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="CLI">
    1. [Install the CLI](/installation) and clone your repository
    2. Edit `index.mdx` in your preferred editor
    3. Run `mint dev` to preview at `http://localhost:3000`
    4. Push your changes to deploy automatically
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
