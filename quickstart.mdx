[Create an account](https://mintlify.com/start) to get started. Your site deploys automatically.

## Make your first edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the editor">
    Go to the [Editor](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/editor)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a page">
    Select `index.mdx` and change the title
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** to deploy your changes
  </Step>
</Steps>
