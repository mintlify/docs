Admin contact emails are the addresses Mintlify uses to send administrative notifications about your organization, including deployment alerts and account-level updates.

If you don't set any admin contact emails, Mintlify falls back to the email address of the oldest user in your organization.

## Configure admin contact emails

To add, update, or remove admin contact emails, go to the [Admin contact emails](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/organization/admin-contact-emails) page of your dashboard.

You can list multiple email addresses to notify more than one person. Each address must be a valid, deliverable email.
