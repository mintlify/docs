<Tip>
  Limit admin access to only the people who need to perform admin tasks.
</Tip>

## Viewer role in the web editor

Viewers can open the web editor to browse content and leave feedback without the ability to edit or publish. When a viewer opens the editor:

- **Suggesting mode is always active**. Viewers can propose text changes as [suggestions](/editor/suggestions) that editors and admins can accept or reject. Suggesting mode cannot be turned off for viewers.
- **Comments are available**. Viewers can leave [comments](/editor/comments) on any text selection.
- **Navigation and configuration are read-only**. Viewers can browse pages and view settings but cannot create, rename, delete, or reorder pages. Configuration settings open in read-only mode.
- **Publishing is disabled**. The **Publish** button is hidden for viewers.
- **Media uploads are disabled**. Viewers cannot upload images or videos.

## Add members to your organization

By default, the person who creates your Mintlify organization has admin access. Add additional editors in the [Members](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/organization/members) page of your dashboard.

You can have as many members as you need in your organization. Seats can incur additional costs. See the [pricing page](https://mintlify.com/pricing) for more details.
