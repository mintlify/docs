Use the Notifications page to control which email addresses receive transactional and security communications about your organization, including billing alerts, deployment notifications, and vulnerability reports.

## Admin contact emails

Admin contacts receive operational communications about your organization, such as:

- Billing alerts and usage overages
- Deployment notifications, including deletions
- Other account-level updates

By default, the longest-tenured admin in your organization receives these emails. Add one or more admin contacts to override that default and route communications to the right people, such as a shared inbox or a dedicated billing address.

<Info>
  At least one admin contact email is required. You cannot remove the last remaining address.
</Info>

### Configure admin contact emails

1. Open the [Notifications](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/organization/notifications) page in your dashboard.
2. In the **Admin contact emails** section, enter an email address.
3. Select **Add email** to add additional recipients.
4. Select **Save**.

To remove a contact, select the trash icon next to the email address and then select **Save**.

## Security contact

<Info>
  Security contact is available on [Pro and Enterprise plans](https://mintlify.com/pricing?ref=security-contact).
</Info>

The security contact receives security-related communications about your organization, including vulnerability reports, security alerts, and compliance updates.

### Configure a security contact

1. Open the [Notifications](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/settings/organization/notifications) page in your dashboard.
2. In the **Security contact email** section, enter an email address.
3. Select **Save**.

To remove the security contact, select **Remove**.
